## What You'll Learn

### Key Concepts

1. **Panic is not dangerous** (though it feels that way)
2. **Avoidance feeds anxiety** (exposure reduces it)
3. **You can't control panic** (but you can change your response)
4. **Paradoxical acceptance** (stopping the fight is the first step)
5. **Recovery is not linear** (and that's okay)
